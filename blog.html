US Embassy hands over classroom block to Avetakpo

The United States Embassy has officially handed over a newly constructed classroom block to the Avetakpo community, providing a safe and modern learning environment for local students. This initiative strengthens educational access, supports youth development, and reflects the Embassy’s commitment to fostering community growth in Ghana.

Write for World initiates project targeted at eradicating poverty-induced social vices

Write for World has launched a transformative project aimed at tackling poverty-driven social vices by empowering vulnerable communities with education, vocational training, and mentorship. The initiative seeks to reduce unemployment, inspire positive youth engagement, and create sustainable pathways out of poverty, fostering safer and more resilient societies.

Write for World appoints new board chairman

Write for World has appointed Korsi Bodja as its new board chairman, bringing his experience in leadership and philanthropy to guide the organisation’s mission. His two-year tenure will focus on innovative policies and strengthening community programs, a commitment highlighted during the annual general meeting.

About Us

Empowering youth through education, health, leadership, and sustainability.

Overview

Write for World is a youth-focused non-profit organisation operating in Ghana, with a mission to empower young people through education, health, leadership development, entrepreneurship and environmental sustainability. Established to bridge gaps in youth development, the organisation has impacted communities in the Oti and Volta Regions through innovative projects and grassroots engagement.

Through innovative community engagement and purpose-driven storytelling, the organisation amplifies the voices of the underserved and mobilizes support to address systemic challenges. Write for World redefines community service by nurturing dreams, restoring hope, and building bridges between vulnerable populations and the global community.

Vision

To create a better world for youth through lasting educational support for needy children and sustainable economic empowerment for young and at-risk girls.

Mission

To harness the collective expertise of members in Education, Research and Entrepreneurship to change lives and build resilient communities.

Unique Approach

Write for World uses the power of positive and purpose-driven writing to spotlight the struggles and aspirations of marginalized youth. By telling their stories to the world, the organization seeks to inspire action, attract support and foster a sense of national and global solidarity.

Impact

Since its inception, Write for World has reached hundreds of children through literacy campaigns, donations of goods to schools, orphanages and school build project, engaged young girls and youth in mentorship and entrepreneurial skills training, supported the youth, particularly the female youth in project communities with economic empowerment tools to enhance livelihoods, created platforms for youth expression through writing and storytelling and built partnerships with local communities to sustain outreach efforts.

Key Achievements

Vocational Skills Training

Trained over 500 youth in vocational skills across the Volta Region and Oti Region

Creative Writing & Literacy

Supported 20 schools with creative writing and literacy initiatives in Volta and Oti Regions.

Community Health Outreach

Conducted community health outreach programmes reaching more than 5,000 people;

Youth Participation Advocacy

Advocated for youth participation in district governance fora.

Community Classroom Project

Completed a 3-Unit Community Classroom Block at Avetakpo in the Ho-West District of the Volta Region, Ghana

Vocational Training Centre

Refurbished a Vocational Training Centre(Centre for Vocation and Trade Project) in Ho Municipality, Volta Region, Ghana.

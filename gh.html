About Us

Write for World is a nonprofit registered in Ghana in 2019. We empower disadvantaged children and youth through education, storytelling, and community outreach.

Vision

To improve the world of the youth through sustainable support and empowerment.

Mission

Education, Outreach, Patriotism, Storytelling, and Community Change.

Core Values

Selflessness, Commitment, Respect, Passion

Our Programs

Education & Literacy

Community education and literacy support for underserved youth.

Storytelling Workshops

Empowering youth to write and share their stories.

Girls’ Empowerment

Support and mentorship for vulnerable and at-risk girls.

Cultural Engagement

Promoting peace, patriotism, and tourism through youth-led initiatives.

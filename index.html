About Us

Write for World is a youth-focused non-profit organisation operating in Ghana, with a mission to empower young people through education, health, leadership development, entrepreneurship and environmental sustainability. Established to bridge gaps in youth development, the organisation has impacted communities in the Oti and Volta Regions through innovative projects and grassroots engagement

Vision

To create a better world for youth through lasting educational support for needy children and sustainable economic empowerment for young and at-risk girls.

Mission

To harness the collective expertise of members in Education, Research and Entrepreneurship to change lives and build resilient communities.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

We value your contribution no matter the form it takes. At Write for the World, the impact of our quest to transform the lives of the needy and vulnerable greatly depends on your support. You can make a lasting impact by doing the following:

Make a Generous Donation

Kindly visit our secure portal at www.writeforworld.org to make a one-time or recurring contribution providing support for needy children and vulnerable young and at-risk girls.

Arrange a Monthly Donation

Your continuous support ensures we sustain our pro-poor activities. Your regular donation empowers vulnerable girls and supports needy children.

Become a Volunteer

Join our team if you share our vision and make direct contributions to our initiatives. Your time and professional skills enhance the impact of our activities.

Host a Fundraising Event

Walk the passion by mobilizing your community for fundraising events such as charity walks, storytelling nights, or cultural showcases.

About Us

Write for World is a nonprofit registered in Ghana in 2019. We empower disadvantaged children and youth through education, storytelling, and community outreach.

  • Vision: To improve the world of the youth through sustainable support and empowerment.
  • Mission: Education, Outreach, Patriotism, Storytelling, and Community Change.
  • Core Values: Selflessness, Commitment, Respect, Passion
